Homemade Blackberry Lemonade

Homemade lemonade is easy to make and gets a flavor boost from blackberry. The end result is light and refreshing which is perfect for those hot summer days(Yes I need something like this for my summers).

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar.
  • 4 1/2 cups water divided.
  • 1 cup blackberries.
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar.
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice.
  • Ice cubes.

Steps

  1. Heat 3/4 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water in a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until sugar has dissolved, about 2 minutes. Let cool.
  2. Place blackberries and remaining 2 tablespoon of sugar in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  3. Combine simple syrup, blended blackberries, remaining 4 cup of water, and lemon juice. Place a fine-mesh sieve over a pitcher and pour lemonade into the pitcher. Discard solids. Serve over ice.
